HOLO Portal Surgical Guidance System

The HOLO Portal project is to pioneer a cutting-edge surgical guidance system that harnesses the capabilities of AI and AR to enhance surgical outcomes, streamline procedures, and empower surgeons with real-time 3D visualization during surgeries.

HOLO™ AI Insights for Spine Imaging

HOLO AI Insights leverages Surgalign's advanced technology to automate the analysis of medical images securely via a scalable cloud platform. It focuses on analyzing lumbar spine MRI images, providing quantitative measurements for 16 spinal structures.

Electron Resonance Imaging System

ERI, or Electron Resonance Imaging, leverages Electron Paramagnetic Resonance principles to explore the complex physiology of living organisms. By combining a tailored magnetic field with spin probes, typically nitroxides or trityls, ERI reveals vital biological data: absolute oxygen levels, redox states, pH changes, and oxidative stress. This breakthrough technology has far-reaching implications for clinical and research applications.

Open-Source Infusion Syringe Pump

Project focuses on creating an open-source single infusion syringe pump tailored for biomedical imaging labs. Unlike expensive commercial options with limited functionality, our device offers adaptability to meet individual experiment needs. It's controlled by an Arduino Leonardo and a stepper motor driver, with command communication facilitated through a **C#** software via USB. Additionally, a user-friendly, open-source GUI is included.

cRECOv: 3D and 4D Image Reconstruction and Visualization Software

cRECOv project focuses on 3D and 4D image reconstruction procedures. Developed algorithms and accompanying tools, such as projection interpolation and various projection filtering methods were made to create software capable of reconstructing and visualizing 3D and 4D EPR images obtained from the EPR Elexsys E540 L tomograph. The entire set of procedures and algorithms is enclosed within a using MATLAB.
